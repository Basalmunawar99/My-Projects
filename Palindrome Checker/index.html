Palindrome Checker



A palindrome is a word, phrase, or sequence that reads the same forwards and backwards,
ignoring spaces, punctuation, and capitalization.
Common examples include "racecar," "level," and "A man, a plan, a canal: Panama!"
Palindromes demonstrate the playful nature of language and can be found in various forms,
from simple words to complex sentences.
They challenge our perception of symmetry and can be a fun linguistic puzzle to solve.
